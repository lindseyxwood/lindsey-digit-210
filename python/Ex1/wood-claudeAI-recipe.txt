Creamy Chicken and Mushrooms

Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 cloves garlic, minced 
- 1 pound chicken breasts, diced
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced 
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1 cup almond milk
- 3 eggs
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute. 

2. Add the chicken and cook, stirring occasionally, until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Transfer to a plate and set aside. 

3. Add the mushrooms to the skillet and cook for 5 minutes, until softened. 

4. Whisk together the almond milk and eggs in a bowl. Pour into the skillet with the mushrooms. 

5. Add the cooked chicken, cucumber, salt and pepper back into the skillet. Cook, stirring gently, until the eggs are set, about 3-5 minutes. 

6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra pepper if desired. Enjoy!

Chicken and Mushroom Casserole

Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 cucumbers, sliced 
- 1 cup almond milk
- 6 eggs
- 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded 
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Grease a 9x13 casserole dish with 1 tablespoon of the butter. 

2. In a skillet, sauté the mushrooms in the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter until soft, about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

3. In a large bowl, beat together the eggs and almond milk. Stir in the cooked chicken, cucumbers, mushrooms, breadcrumbs, parmesan, thyme, salt and pepper.

4. Pour the mixture into the prepared casserole dish. Bake for 45-50 minutes until set and top is golden brown. 

5. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.

Enjoy this hearty and delicious chicken casserole! The almond milk gives it a nice creamy texture while the cucumbers add freshness. Let me know if you try this recipe!

Cucumber Chicken Pot Pies

Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cucumber, diced 
- 1 cup almond milk
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded 
- 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
- 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F.

2. In a skillet over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the cucumber and mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes until softened. 

3. In a bowl, whisk together the almond milk and eggs. Add the chicken, mushroom mixture, salt and pepper.

4. Cut the puff pastry into four squares. Press each square into a ramekin or small oven-safe dish. Spoon the chicken mixture evenly into the dough-lined ramekins. 

5. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the pastry is golden brown and the filling is set. 

6. Let cool slightly before removing from the ramekins. Serve warm.

The unique flaire here is using the cucumber to add freshness and moisture to the pot pie filling. The individual pot pie shape in ramekins gives it an elegant, unique presentation as well. Enjoy this fun twist on chicken pot pie!
